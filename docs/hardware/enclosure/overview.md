## 3D Printed Nodebox

The official TARPN enclosure designed by Tadd Torborg (KA2DEW).

**Features:**

- Modular snap-fit design
- Holds 4 radios and 4 NinoTNCs
- Integrated ventilation
- Tool-free panel removal

## Components

- 97 total printed parts
- ~58 hours print time
- PLA or PETG material
