## Recommended Radios

| Radio | Band | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Tait TM8100/8200 | VHF/UHF | Excellent, surplus available |
| Motorola CDM1250 | VHF/UHF | Common, reliable |
| Yaesu FTM-3100R | 2m | New, affordable |
| Vertex VX-2200 | VHF/UHF | Commercial grade |

## Requirements

- FM transceiver (VHF or UHF)
- 9600 baud capable (for high-speed links)
- Flat audio response preferred
- 25-50W output typical

## Wiring

Connect radio to NinoTNC via audio cable:

- TX Audio (TNC to radio mic input)
- RX Audio (radio speaker to TNC)
- PTT (active low)
- Ground
