!!! note "Documentation In Progress"

## Quick Summary

For a complete 4-port TARPN node, you'll need:

- 1x Raspberry Pi 4B (2GB+)
- 4x NinoTNC N9600A
- 4x VHF/UHF Radio (Tait, Motorola, or Yaesu)
- 1x 13.8V Power Supply (20A+)
- 4x Antennas
- Hardware fasteners (see [McMaster-Carr list](../hardware/bom/mcmaster-carr.md))
