### Recommended Radios

**Kenwood (1200 baud):**
- TK-760/760H (VHF) - $30-50
- TK-860/860H (UHF) - $30-50
- TK-862G (UHF, 8ch) - $40-60

**Vertex (6m capable):**
- FTL-1011 (50 MHz) - $35-70
- FTL-2011 (VHF) - $35-70

**Tait (9600 baud capable):**
- TM8105 - $50-80
