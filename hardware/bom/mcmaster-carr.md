## What You Get

After this order, you'll have:

- **100x** M2.5 brass hex nuts
- **50x** M4 x 10mm flanged socket head screws
- **200x** M3 thin-profile square nuts (the key fastener for the enclosure)
- **50x** M3 x 65mm stainless socket head screws
- **25x** M3 x 40mm black socket head screws
- **200x** M3 x 10mm zinc socket head screws
- **50x** M2.5 x 14mm black socket head screws
- **3x** Hex screwdrivers (2mm, 2.5mm, 3mm)
- **1x** Ball-end hex bit for power tools

## Notes

- The M3 square nuts (97259A101) are the most critical - you need ~60 per enclosure
- Two packs of 100 gives you enough for one enclosure with plenty of spares
- The 10mm M3 screws are used most frequently - 4 packs recommended
- Tools are one-time purchase
